Disadvantages

  • Frames are Not "Search Engine Friendly"
  • Frames are Not "URL/Bookmark Friendly"
  • Frames are Not as Accessible
  • Broken Framesets
  • The user's back button often doesn't work.
  • Frames often reduce the amount of usable space on the page.
  • The URL in the address bar always stays the same. Although some webmasters do this on purpose, it is generally considered undesirable for experienced web users.
  • Frames create problems with printing.
